About

I'm a software engineer, indie hacker, and builder. I specialize in building full-stack web applications, marketing-driven tools, and SaaS platforms that attract users and generate revenue.
My work lives at the intersection of engineering and entrepreneurship. I don’t just code — I build real-world products, optimize for user growth, and treat every project as a case study in product-market fit. Whether it's syncing data with CRMs like HubSpot, gamifying fitness, or building internal tools, I focus on quality, speed, and outcomes.

Recent Works

Here are some of my favorite projects I have done lately. Feel free to check them out.

  • ClientSync
    A lightweight CRM that connects with HubSpot to sync contacts, deals, pipelines, and workflows. Includes OAuth integration, custom automation rules, and real-time webhook support — built for agencies that need full control over their client data.
  • SolarCalculator
    A solar cost-savings calculator that estimates energy savings and ROI for users installing solar panels. Users input their location, energy usage, and roof size to get instant insights into installation costs, payback period, and monthly savings.
  • FitnessQuest
    A Duolingo-style fitness app that guides users through structured daily workout programs. Tracks progress, awards XP and levels, and adapts to the user’s fitness journey — making consistency fun and measurable.
  • SocialScheduler
    A social media content scheduler for creators and businesses. Lets users plan, organize, and auto-post across Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more — complete with a calendar view and post templates.
